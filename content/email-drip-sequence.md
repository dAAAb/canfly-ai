## Technical Notes (for implementation)

### Trigger
- Newsletter subscription form on homepage (`newsletter` section)
- Signup confirmation triggers the drip sequence

### Sending Schedule
| Email | Delay | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1. Welcome | Immediate | First touch, link to free tutorial |
| 2. Flight Plan | +24 hours | Map the upgrade path |
| 3. Advanced | +72 hours | Showcase premium features (affiliate CTAs) |
| 4. Community | +168 hours | Retention & community building |

### Tracking & Affiliate Links
- Email 2 & 3 contain affiliate product links (Zeabur, ElevenLabs, HeyGen)
- All links should include UTM parameters: `?utm_source=email&utm_medium=drip&utm_campaign=welcome_series&utm_content=email{N}`
- Affiliate codes embedded per PROJECT.md specs

### Tech Stack (TBD)
Options to evaluate:
- **Resend** — developer-friendly email API, React Email templates
- **Loops** — purpose-built for drip sequences, no-code automation
- **ConvertKit** — newsletter-native, good for creators
- **Brevo (Sendinblue)** — free tier generous, automation built-in

### Success Metrics
- Email 1: Open rate > 60% (welcome emails benchmark)
- Email 2: Click-through to Ollama tutorial > 15%
- Email 3: Click-through to premium tutorials > 8%
- Email 4: Community page visits > 5%
- Overall: < 0.5% unsubscribe rate across sequence
