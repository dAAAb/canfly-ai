## Posting Strategy

### Recommended Posting Order
1. **Ollama** (lead with free — lowest barrier)
2. **HeyGen** (most visually impressive)
3. **ElevenLabs** (unique voice angle)
4. **Zeabur** (conversion-focused)
5. **Umbrel** (power user audience)

### Platform-Specific Notes

| Platform | Format | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Twitter/X | Upload as video tweet | Use English caption. Add 🧵 thread with Chinese version |
| YouTube Shorts | Upload vertical MP4 | Title = first line of caption. Description = full caption |
| Instagram Reels | Upload vertical MP4 | Caption in English, Chinese in first comment |
| TikTok | Upload vertical MP4 | Caption in English or Chinese based on audience targeting |

### Posting Cadence
- Post 1 reel every 2-3 days
- Best times: 9am-11am and 7pm-9pm (target timezone)
- Cross-post to all platforms simultaneously
- Engage with comments within first hour

### Universal Hashtags (use on every post)
`#CanFly #AI #AIAssistant #NowYouCanFly`

### CTA Pattern
Always end with a link to the relevant tutorial page on canfly.ai.
